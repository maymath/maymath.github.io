Sunday Session at the Fastnet Pub

Every Sunday from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM, we gather at the Fastnet Pub in Newport, RI for a traditional Irish music session. All musicians are welcome to join in and play along. The session is open to all levels of experience.

The Fastnet Pub is located at

1 Broadway, Newport, RI 02840

For more information about the pub, visit their website: www.fastnetpub.com

Newport, Rhode Island has a rich history of Irish immigration and cultural influence. The Fastnet Pub is named after the Fastnet Rock, a small island off the coast of Ireland. The pub serves traditional Irish food and drink, and hosts live music throughout the week.
