The Sunday Irish Music Session at the Fastnet Pub in Newport, RI

Call The Fastnet Pub in Newport, RI to find out more information: 401-845-9311 or send a message to: banjohopper@gmail.com

The pub is located at 1 Broadway where Aidan's used to be.
It's named for the Fastnet Lighthouse off the coast of Cork, Ireland.
The Fastnet Pub Irish music session started in October 2006 - over 900 Sundays ago! It happens every Sunday year-round from 6-9 PM. Guest musicians, singers, and/or dancers, who are familiar with the traditional repertoire, are always welcome to join in the fun. Families are welcome to take advantage of the tasty pub grub with particularly good fish and chips

Below: "The Fastnet Pub Sunday Irish Session Musicians". This painting was created in 2019 by William Heydt.

This is a vintage photo from 2009 at the Fastnet Pub in Newport. 2 of the musicians have passed away and we hope to see them someday on Fiddler's Green. They are Tom Maguire, and Fud Benson, the first 2 you see from left to right. The rest of us are still here playing away, now 18 years strong on Sunday nights at the Fastnet Pub in Newport.
