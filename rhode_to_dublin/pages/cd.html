Something Old Something New

News from August 2010

Timmy May has recorded a CD entitled "Something Old Something New" with original and traditional tunes and songs. 8 of the 13 tracks on the CD are original tunes paired with traditional tunes. The music was performed and recorded by Timmy on the guitar, banjo, and mandolin.

Send a message to: banjohopper@gmail.com for more information as to how to get this CD.
