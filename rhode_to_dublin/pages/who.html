Who We Are

Rhode To Dublin is a traditional Irish music group based in Newport, Rhode Island. We play a mix of traditional Irish tunes and songs, as well as some contemporary pieces that fit our style.

Our group includes musicians who play various instruments including fiddle, guitar, banjo, mandolin, and bodhran. We welcome musicians of all skill levels to join us at our weekly sessions.
