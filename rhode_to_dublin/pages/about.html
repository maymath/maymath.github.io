Who..

Rhode To Dublin is a group of musicians who play Irish and American traditional music. The group is based in Newport, RI, USA.

Introducing the musicians...

Rhode to Dublin is a revolving cast of musicians.
Timmy May is the founder and manager of the band and also the creator of this website. Rhode to Dublin always includes a variety of fine musicians and singers who play violin, uilleann pipes, whistle, accordeon, guitar, banjo, mandolin, and other instruments. In the photos you can see different configurations of Rhode to Dublin. The band is organized for different types of concerts and events and will customize a set list for any gathering. There is a common element of Irish/Celtic music in the sound.
