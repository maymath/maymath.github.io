Welcome to Rhode To Dublin

Welcome to our website! We are a traditional Irish music group based in Newport, Rhode Island.

Join us every Sunday from 4-7 PM at the Fastnet Pub in Newport, RI for our weekly traditional Irish music session. All musicians are welcome to join in, regardless of skill level!

Newport, Rhode Island has a rich history of Irish immigration and cultural influence. The Fastnet Pub is named after the Fastnet Rock, a small island off the coast of Ireland. The pub serves traditional Irish food and drink, and hosts live music throughout the week.
