Welcome to Rhode To Dublin

Welcome to our website! We are a traditional Irish music group based in Newport, Rhode Island.

We play toe-tapping music for all ages that is both ancient and new. We play for all types of events in all types of venues. Whether it's a party, a wedding ceremony, cocktail party, or reception, we can provide the traditional musical atmosphere you are looking for! If you're looking for fun, family-friendly music that will enliven any gathering, look no further!!
